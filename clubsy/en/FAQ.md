# FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

> **Status:** Public draft
> **Last updated:** 2026-03-03
> **Application:** ClubSy

## 1) What is ClubSy?
ClubSy is a platform to manage clubs, teams, events, attendance, and internal communication.

## 2) How do I create an account?
You can sign up from the initial screen using email (and/or phone, if enabled), complete your profile, and verify your access.

## 3) How do I join a club?
You can receive an invitation from a club via link or token. After accepting it, your profile is linked to that club.

## 4) How do I change my language?
Go to **Settings > Language** and select your preferred language.

## 5) How do I update my profile information?
From **My Profile**, you can update your name, photo, and other allowed fields.

## 6) What if I forgot my password?
On the login screen, use **Reset password** and follow the instructions sent to your email.

## 7) What user roles are available?
Roles (for example: player, coach, management, staff) depend on each club configuration.

## 8) Can I belong to more than one team?
Yes, if the club allows it and assigns multiple memberships to your account.

## 9) How do I report an issue or contact support?
Use **Report issue** inside the app or contact official support:
- Email: support-clubsy@powc2.app

## 10) What devices are supported?
ClubSy is available on mobile platforms and may include additional platform support depending on the published version.
